# Project PRD: Cozy Lagos Lifestyle Ecosystem

## 1. Executive Summary
**Cozy Lagos** is a premium "Lifestyle Commerce Ecosystem" designed for high-net-worth individuals, elite travelers, and luxury residents in Lagos, Nigeria. The platform transcends traditional short-let marketplaces by integrating luxury rentals, bespoke experiences, and professional services under a unified, AI-driven concierge layer.

## 2. Problem Statement
The luxury market in Lagos is fragmented. Discerning clients must navigate multiple platforms or unreliable manual networks to book high-end apartments, secure private chefs, or charter yachts. There is a lack of a single, trustworthy "white-glove" digital interface that manages the entire end-to-end luxury lifestyle.

## 3. Goals & Objectives
*   **Centralization**: Consolidate luxury rentals, services, and experiences into one ecosystem.
*   **Trust & Verification**: Implement rigorous onboarding and verification for all vendors and hosts.
*   **Personalization**: Leverage AI to provide proactive, bespoke recommendations and itinerary planning.
*   **Frictionless Transactions**: Secure payment processing with high-touch human confirmation (WhatsApp Concierge).

## 4. User Personas
### A. The Elite Traveler (Guest)
*   **Profile**: High-income professional or tourist seeking a seamless, luxury stay in Lagos.
*   **Needs**: High-fidelity property visuals, curated local experiences, and 24/7 concierge support.

### B. The Property Owner (Host)
*   **Profile**: Owners of luxury penthouses or villas in Ikoyi, Victoria Island, or Lekki.
*   **Needs**: Professional listing management, booking calendar, and transparent payout tracking.

### C. The Service Provider / Experience Vendor
*   **Profile**: Private chefs, wellness experts, yacht operators, and tour guides.
*   **Needs**: A platform to showcase high-end catalogs and reach a qualified, affluent audience.

## 5. Core Product Pillars & Feature Set

### Pillar 1: Discovery & Booking (Consumer-Facing)
*   **High-Impact Home & Search**: Visual-first exploration of apartments and experiences.
*   **Transparent Checkout**: Split-screen checkout with cost breakdowns and multiple payment methods.
*   **WhatsApp Concierge Verification**: Post-payment manual verification for security and service quality.

### Pillar 2: Elite AI Concierge (Interaction Layer)
*   **Concierge Hub**: Real-time chat interface with rich-media service cards.
*   **Proactive Itinerary Planning**: AI-generated daily schedules based on user preferences.
*   **Service Integration**: Direct booking of providers (chefs, drivers) within the chat.

### Pillar 3: Management Portals (B2B)
*   **Owner Dashboard**: Financial overviews, property management, and calendar synchronization.
*   **Provider Onboarding Wizards**: Multi-step flows for identity verification, catalog creation, and media uploads.

## 6. Technical Requirements
*   **Design System**: Strict adherence to the "Luxury Lagos Lifestyle" system (Gold/Charcoal palette, Playfair Display typography).
*   **AI Integration**: LLM trained on Lagos geography, luxury trends, and the platform’s specific vendor database.
*   **Security**: Encrypted payment gateways and multi-step verification for all ecosystem participants.

## 7. Success Metrics
*   **Booking Conversion Rate**: Percentage of discovery sessions leading to confirmed stays.
*   **Service Attachment Rate**: Average number of professional services booked per property stay.
*   **Vendor Retention**: Growth and stability of the elite service provider network.
